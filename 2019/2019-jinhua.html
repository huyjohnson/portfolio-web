Jinhua Spa and Retreat

2019


The spa and retreat sits within an existing reservoir, newly rezoned as a large tourist park. The location of the site lies awkwardly between two hills, and the project attempts to bridge together the existing landmarks while leaving the landscape untouched. The large entrance canopy celebrates the vast scenic vista just below these two hills, all the while introducing shade and a micro-climate underneath.

Wuhan House

2016


In developing the concept of the Wuhan House, two rather distinct philosophies emerged: that of the visual and that of the phenomenological. This housing complex explores the degrees of visibility and permeability between the interior and the exterior through the manipulation of materiality and voids.

The design intends to create an active experience on every level that transitions between public and the private boundaries. The below ground public experience invites neighoring visitors to explore the partitioned spaces, while the above ground private residences fulfill the security and privacy that is sometimes lost in such a dense and urban environment.

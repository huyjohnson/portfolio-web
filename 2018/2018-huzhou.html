Huzhou CBD Tower

2017


Situated adjacent to Taihu Lake, this new CBD planned by the city government attempts to revitalize what is presently underutilized. The new urban center brings together over a hundred new tech companies and incubator hubs in order to accomodate the new tech revolution along this part of the country. The focal point of this urban core lies a planned 300m tall mixed-use tower, with offices, hotels, and private residences packed together within this density.

Montparnasse Tower Competition

2016-2017, all images courtesy of MAD Architects


In leading the design team for the Montparnasse competition, my roles included delegating more than a dozen architects and interns in developing the overall design strategy for the project as well as maintaining high quality production work over a long design period.

During this time, I joined weekly updates and strategy sessions with the regional executive architects, communicated with international facade and structural consultants, coordinated with international design visualization companies, and coordinated design updates between the partners and the design team. I crafted client presentations and presented in Paris to client representatives and a multidisciplinary competition group.

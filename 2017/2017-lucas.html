Lucas Museum of Narrative Art

2014-2017

Under Construction, Completion 2021


As part of the core design team for the Lucas Museum project, my roles included designing major interior and exterior spaces through 3D modelling, plans, detailing of unique building sections, and physical models.

I was heavily involved in all phases of the project, starting from the original schematic design phase of the Chicago site to the end of design development, then worked on early concept phases for both San Francisco and Los Angeles sites, then completed my involvement through to the end of design development for the Los Angeles site.
